MemoryKloud

MemoryKloud enhances users' experience for memory preservation. MemoryKloud consists of a mobile application, which would sit in between users and social media platforms to record interesting moments based on events, and a web application that could potentially pull users' updates from various social platforms and auto-classify them into relevant events.
